Homepage direction review board

Three homepage directions, compared clearly.

This board is prepared for client review. It compares three distinct homepage directions: a commerce-forward route, a premium editorial route, and a balanced recommended route. All product, image, and brand examples have been neutralised so the board focuses on design logic rather than any example brand.

Direction A

Direction B

Direction C

Category-first and concern-first shopping

This route is the strongest option for speed, shopper orientation, and scalable search-ready merchandising. It is the most direct path into ecommerce operations.
